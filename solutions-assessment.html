Purpose
Assessment insights are the foundation for talent practices that support the optimal deployment, leveraging, and growth of your critical leadership talent. Our assessments are designed to:
Reshape talent conversations and redefine potential.
Challenge assumptions and models that impede growth.
Empower leaders to serve as stewards of their talent.
Inspire leaders to own and accelerate their own growth.
Target development that brings out the best in all people.
Discover our assessments
We offer four tiers of assessments designed to target the needs at different points in your pipeline, with more depth and nuance at the top and more speed and efficiency deeper in the pipeline.
Winning Formula®
CEO Succession and C-Suite. Our most customized, high-touch assessment built to answer your specific and contextual questions. Combines robust multi-source data from interviews, stakeholder input, and psychometric instruments.
RFSexec
C-Suite and 1-Away. Combines role-based and contextual questions with RHR’s standardized model of scalable leadership, Readiness for ScaleSM. Combines robust multi-source data from interviews and psychometric instruments with an optional 360.
RFSpipeline
Next generation. Focused on gathering broad insights on your pipeline of next generation leaders for succession and development. Leverages a standardized, globally benchmarked assessment of scalable leadership (Readiness for ScaleSM). Combines robust multi-source data from interviews and psychometric instruments with an optional 360.
RFSinsight
Deep bench. Our most efficient and scalable assessment. Built to scan deeper in your pipeline for future leaders and hidden talent. Supports talent segmentation and cohort identification. Fully automated assessment conducted through an online business simulation and 360.
